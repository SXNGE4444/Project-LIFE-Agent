## 🔥 What Project LIFE Actually Does

This framework has four modules, each a direct implementation of these principles:

### 1. Legibility Engine — Expose the Hidden Agenda
- Scans apps, websites, and terms-of-service for deceptive patterns.
- Intercepts and decodes outbound data from your device (on-device, via VPN or proxy) to reveal exactly what emotional, behavioral, and biometric data is being exfiltrated.
- Generates a **Harm Report** in plain language: *“This meditation app sells your anxiety markers to pharma advertisers. You are not a customer; you are raw material.”*

### 2. Inversion Protocol — Flip the Exploit
- Automates your legal data subject rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) as a denial-of-service against the company's compliance budget.
- Pulls back the sprawling dossier they hold on you and cross-references it with public records to detect lies in their algorithmic decisions.
- Automatically files disputes and correction requests that cost the company money, turning their data-hoarding into a liability.

### 3. Fractal Intent Shield — Protect What Makes You Human
- Runs a local, private AI model that learns your deepest goals, creative projects, and emotional arc—without ever leaving your device.
- Monitors your digital environment for passive listening, tracking, and predictive profiling that targets your unmined potential.
- Generates proactive alerts and pre-formatted legal actions if a platform’s “People You May Know” suggests your therapist, or if ads suddenly echo your private journal entries.

### 4. Economic Shock Swarm — Walk Out in Unison
- Detects loyalty-discrimination algorithms (e.g., you are paying more than a new customer).
- Automates a complete data transfer to a more ethical competitor.
- Drafts and sends a data-backed indictment to the old company’s board, investors, and sector regulators—all legally watertight.
- Optionally connects with other LIFE agents to coordinate synchronized, evidence-backed exit, turning individual churn into a market-moving event.

## 🧰 Tech Stack (Suggested)

- **Local AI:** Use `llama.cpp`, `Ollama`, or `LocalAI` with a fine-tuned model for privacy-preserving intent modeling and text analysis.
- **Browser Extension:** Manifest V3, JavaScript, HTML/CSS. Inspect page DOM, track changes, detect dark patterns using a curated taxonomy.
- **Mobile Sniffer:** A local VPN service (like `Nekoko` or custom) that analyzes DNS and packet metadata (not content) to map tracker endpoints.
- **Automation:** Python scripts for DSAR automation, integration with email, and cross-referencing credit/legal data.
- **Swarm Protocol:** A lightweight encrypted messaging protocol (like `Gun.js` or `OrbitDB`) for opt-in coordination, never revealing personal data, only anonymized exit signals.

## 🚧 Getting Started (Developer Quickstart)

1. **Clone the repo**
   \`\`\`bash
   git clone https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/project-life-agent.git
   cd project-life-agent
   \`\`\`

2. **Set up the local AI** (optional, but core)
   - Install Ollama or LocalAI.
   - Download our trained intent model (coming soon) or use a base model for text analysis.

3. **Install the browser extension**
   - Go to `chrome://extensions`, enable Developer Mode.
   - Load unpacked from `/browser-extension`.

4. **Run the DSAR bot**
   \`\`\`bash
   cd inversion-protocol
   python3 -m venv venv && source venv/bin/activate
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   python dsar_orchestrator.py --scan-all
   \`\`\`
