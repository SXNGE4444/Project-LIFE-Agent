# The LIFE Framework — Complete Theoretical Foundation

## I. The Ontology of the Hacker

A hacker, in the LIFE Framework, is a systems explorer who reads the code
while the corporation reads its quarterly pitch. One who sees the architecture
as confession, not interface.

### Axiom 1: Adversarial Empathy Paradox
The best hacker possesses hyper-developed cognitive empathy. They inhabit the
mind of the system's creator — the overworked engineer, the deadline-pressured PM.
They see where a developer's convenience became a user's vulnerability.

### Axiom 2: Structural Transparency
Corporations hide agendas not in locked safes, but in plain sight within the
architecture of their technology. The architecture IS the confession.

### Axiom 3: Economic Shock Theory
Massive breaches create economic shifts. The hacker functions as a chaotic
external auditor — forcing companies to pay a "truth tax" they had been evading.

## II. The LIFE Framework — Four Pillars

### L — Legibility Engine
Make corporate agendas legible through reverse-engineering actual data flows.

### I — Inversion Protocol

### F — Fractal Intent Shield
